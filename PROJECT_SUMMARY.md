# Project Summary: ChronaTrace (Aiventra)
**AI-Powered Forensic Triage & Postmortem Intelligence System**

---

### 1. Problem Statement
Investigative agencies and forensic departments face significant bottlenecks when processing vast amounts of forensic and digital evidence. Manual analysis of unstructured autopsy reports, environmental conditions, and digital traces (CCTV logs, mobile metadata) is time-consuming and prone to human error or oversight. Complex cases often lack a unified system to correlate these disparate data sources, leading to delays in early-stage triage and critical investigative leads.

### 2. Our Solution: ChronaTrace
ChronaTrace is an intelligent forensic support system designed to assist investigators by organizing, analyzing, and correlating forensic and digital evidence in real-time. It acts as a **Forensic Operational Center**, providing a unified high-tech interface that bridges the gap between traditional postmortem indicators and modern digital forensics.

### 3. Key Unique Features
*   **AI-Based Autopsy Analysis**: Utilizing NLP to extract critical forensic details (cause of death, injury patterns, medical observations) from unstructured text reports.
*   **Predictive TOD Estimation**: An AI-driven calculator that approximates the Time of Death using environmental factors and postmortem indicators (Body/Ambient temp, Rigor Mortis, Livor Mortis).
*   **Digital Evidence Correlation Matrix**: A dynamic mindmap and timeline that connects CCTV timestamps, mobile pings, and geolocation records to identify suspicious patterns.
*   **Case Risk Scoring & Anomaly Detection**: Automated flagging of investigative inconsistencies (e.g., CCTV gaps, alibi discrepancies) with an AI-generated risk score.
*   **Interactive Investigation Dashboard**: A high-fidelity, unified visualization suite providing summarized reports, evidence timelines, and AI-driven predictive scenarios.
*   **Two-Factor Forensic Authentication**: A secure access portal featuring a biometric fingerprint scanner and encrypted PIN verification.

### 4. Tech Stack
*   **Frontend**: React.js with TypeScript (for robust, scalable codebase)
*   **Styling**: Tailwind CSS (Clinical High-Tech / Cyberpunk UI system)
*   **Animations**: Framer Motion (for immersive, smooth interface transitions)
*   **Data Visualization**: React Flow (for evidence correlation mindmapping)
*   **Icons**: Lucide React (Forensic & Security iconography)
*   **AI Integration**: Natural Language Processing (NLP) for report triage and predictive modeling for TOD estimation.

### 5. Existing Problems in the Field
*   **Information Silos**: Forensic data and digital evidence are often stored and analyzed in separate, incompatible systems.
*   **Delayed Triage**: The time between evidence collection and actionable leads is currently measured in days/weeks due to manual report processing.
*   **Cognitive Overload**: Investigators must manually track hundreds of variables across multiple data sources, increasing the risk of missing subtle anomalies.
*   **Lack of Explainability**: Many existing analytical tools act as "black boxes," making it difficult to present AI-derived leads in a structured, professional context.

---
**Conclusion**
ChronaTrace (Aiventra) reduces case delays, improves evidence handling efficiency, and provides investigators with a stronger, more informed foundation for decision-making in complex forensic environments.
